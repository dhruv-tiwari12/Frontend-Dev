Top 3 Travel Destinations

1. Paris, France

Paris, the capital of France, is a global center for art, fashion, and culture. Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine.

"An unforgettable experience. The art and the food were simply magical."

2. Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto, once the capital of Japan, is a city famous for its classical Buddhist temples, as well as gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wood houses.
